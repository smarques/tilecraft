# How to Play Tilecraft

Tilecraft is a word puzzle played on a 6×6 grid. Your goal is to **connect tiles that feel meaningful together** — and explain why.

---
## Start the game

Click on "Start" and enter your full name

## Shuffling

Click **Shuffle** to reset the board and load a new set of random words. Any progress is lost.

## Moving tiles

Switch to **Move mode** (button in the control bar, or press `M`).

Click any tile directly next to the empty space to slide it in. You can also use the arrow keys:

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `↑` | Slide the tile above the empty space down |
| `↓` | Slide the tile below the empty space up |
| `←` | Slide the tile to the left of the empty space right |
| `→` | Slide the tile to the right of the empty space left |

Each slide counts as one move.

## Connecting tiles

Switch to **Highlight mode** (button in the control bar, or press `H`).

Click tiles to select them. You can also click and drag to select several at once. Selected tiles must stay connected — if a tile loses all its neighbours it is automatically deselected.

The more tiles you connect into a meaningful combination, the higher your score.


## Scoring

```
Score = moves + (highlighted tiles × 50)
```

## Saving

Click **Save** while at least one tile is highlighted. You will be asked to write a short sentence explaining your word combination. Your entry — name, score, board snapshot, and statement — is added to the leaderboard, and a PNG of the board is downloaded automatically.

## Seeing other people's choices

Open the menu (hamburger icon, top right) and select **High Scores** to browse everyone's saved combinations, scores, and statements.
